# FitX — Customer Journey Maps
**Version:** 1.0.0

---

## Journey 1: Ahmed (Free User) — From Discovery to Habit

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Touchpoint:** TikTok video showing FitX's "Fridge Rescue" feature with Egyptian food  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "ده التطبيق ده شاطر — بيعرف كشري وفراخ؟!"  
**Emotion:** 😮 Surprised & Curious  
**FitX Action:** Viral TikTok content featuring relatable Egyptian food scenarios  
**Metric:** Video view → app install conversion rate

---

### Stage 2: Download & First Open
**Touchpoint:** Play Store listing → Download → App open  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "يتقل ولا يفتح بسرعة؟"  
**Emotion:** 😐 Skeptical  
**FitX Action:** <40MB app size; <2s cold start; clean welcome screen  
**Friction Points:** App size concern on mobile data  
**Mitigation:** Display "Lightweight app — works great on any phone" in store listing

---

### Stage 3: Onboarding
**Touchpoint:** 7-step onboarding flow  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "شوية أسئلة بس — عادي" → "هو عمل ليا خطة أصلاً؟!"  
**Emotion:** 😊 Engaged → 🤩 Delighted at plan reveal  
**FitX Action:** Progress bar; conversational tone in Arabic; satisfying plan reveal animation  
**Drop Risk:** Onboarding too long → skip button always visible  
**Metric:** Onboarding completion rate target ≥80%

---

### Stage 4: First Workout
**Touchpoint:** Home screen → Today's Workout → Session  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "ده بيقولي أعمل إيه بالظبط — بسيط!"  
**Emotion:** 😊 Confident (no longer intimidated)  
**FitX Action:** Clear illustrations; Arabic exercise names; encouragement copy  
**Win Moment:** Session complete screen with "أحسنت! +50 نقطة"  
**Metric:** First workout completion rate target ≥60% within 24h of install

---

### Stage 5: Food Logging (Day 2–7)
**Touchpoint:** Nutrition tab → Camera → Koshari recognized  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "عرف كشري؟! ده سحر!"  
**Emotion:** 🤩 Impressed → 😊 Engaged  
**FitX Action:** Accurate Egyptian food DB; instant recognition; fun copy ("آه ده كشري أحمد!")  
**Friction:** User might not log every meal  
**Mitigation:** Gamification points for logging; gentle nudge notification at meal times

---

### Stage 6: Discovery of Budget Planner (Week 2)
**Touchpoint:** In-app prompt: "عندك 300 جنيه أسبوعياً؟ شوفنا عملنا إيه!"  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "هيعملي خطة بـ 300 جنيه؟ جرب!"  
**Emotion:** 🤩 Excited  
**FitX Action:** Budget planner output with real Egyptian market prices + shopping list  
**Win Moment:** "بكده بياخد X جرام بروتين يومياً بـ 300 جنيه بس"  
**Metric:** Budget planner usage rate; plan save rate

---

### Stage 7: Community Hook (Week 3)
**Touchpoint:** Gym Mayor leaderboard; Workout Buddy suggestion  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "أنا تاني في الترتيب — لازم أكمل!"  
**Emotion:** 🔥 Competitive; 👥 Belonging  
**FitX Action:** Push notification: "أنت في المركز التاني! واحد بس قبالك — هتسيبه يفوز؟"  
**Metric:** Leaderboard interaction rate; 30-day retention for competitive users vs. non

---

### Stage 8: Month 2 — Habit Formed (or Churn)
**Two paths:**

**Path A — Habit Formed:**
Ahmed is checking in 4x/week, logging meals daily, ranked top 5 in his gym.  
**FitX Action:** Celebrate 30-day streak; surface Pro upsell: "الكاميرا الذكية هتحميك من الإصابة"  
**Emotion:** 💪 Proud → Considering upgrade

**Path B — Risk of Churn:**
Ahmed misses 5 days. Streak broken.  
**FitX Action:** Voice coach message: "فين يا بطل؟ الجيم بيتسألك عليك!"  
**Recovery:** Emergency workout suggestion; streak forgiveness mechanic (1x/month)

---

### Stage 9: Pro Upgrade Consideration
**Trigger:** Ahmed sees AI pose correction promo; or friend uses Pro  
**Ahmed's Thought:** "39 جنيه بس في الشهر؟ ده أقل من أوبر واحدة."  
**Emotion:** 🤔 Calculating  
**FitX Action:** 7-day Pro trial; ROI calculator ("Pro بيوفرلك 200 EGP من الخصومات")  
**Metric:** Trial start rate; trial-to-paid conversion rate
