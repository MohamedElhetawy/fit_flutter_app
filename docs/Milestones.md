## Phase 4: MENA Expansion (Months 19–24)
**Theme: "Egypt first, then the region."**

| Market | Entry Strategy | Timeline |
|--------|---------------|----------|
| Saudi Arabia | Localize food DB + partner with local gyms | Month 19 |
| UAE | Premium positioning; English + Arabic | Month 21 |
| Jordan | Similar to Egypt model | Month 23 |

**Platform changes required:**
- Multi-currency support (SAR, AED, JOD + EGP)
- Multi-dialect voice coach (Khaleeji, Levantine)
- Regional food databases (Saudi local dishes, etc.)
- Country-specific merchant networks
