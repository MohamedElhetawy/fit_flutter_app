## Persona 1: Ahmed — "المبتدئ" (The Beginner)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  👤 Ahmed Mostafa, 21 years old                                  │
│  University Student — Computer Science, Cairo University         │
│  Free User Tier                                                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Background
Ahmed is a second-year CS student living in a shared apartment in Dokki. He spends most of his time sitting — studying or gaming. He's noticed he's gained weight and wants to get fit before summer, but he's intimidated by the gym and doesn't know where to start. His monthly allowance is 1,500 EGP, most of which goes to rent and food.

### Goals
- Lose 8–10 kg before summer
- Build some visible muscle definition
- Learn how to eat healthy without spending more than 300 EGP/week on food
- Avoid looking like a beginner at the gym

### Frustrations
- "The apps I've tried are all in English and they tell me to eat salmon and avocado — where am I supposed to find that?"
- "I tried going to the gym but no one helped me and I think I hurt my shoulder doing something wrong."
- "I get bored of the same routine after two weeks and I stop."
- "I'm scared of those protein supplement ads — I don't know what's real and what's garbage."

### Technology Behavior
- Primary device: Redmi Note 11 (Android)
- Uses TikTok 2+ hours/day
- Discovers apps through friends and TikTok
- Uninstalls apps immediately if they're slow or confusing
- Pays for nothing if a free version exists

### What FitX Solves for Ahmed
- Free beginner workout plans with visual form guides
- Egyptian food recognition for koshari, foul, tamiya
- Budget protein planner: "300 EGP budget → here's your weekly plan"
- Community check-in competition (Gym Mayor) to stay motivated
- Emergency workout for days he can't go to gym

### Quote
> "عايز أبقى fit بدون ما أعمل قرض أو آكل حاجة مش هلاقيها في الكوشة."

## Persona 2: Sara — "المحترفة" (The Professional)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  👤 Sara Hassan, 28 years old                                    │
│  Marketing Specialist — New Cairo                                │
│  Pro User Tier                                                   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Background
Sara works 9–6 at a digital agency in Fifth Settlement. She used to be very active in college but her busy work schedule has made consistent fitness almost impossible. She's willing to pay for a premium service if it respects her time and gives real results. She's tried MyFitnessPal but gave up after being told to eat foods she couldn't find locally.

### Goals
- Get back to her pre-job fitness level
- Train efficiently: maximum results in 20–30 minutes/day
- Track her macros precisely without spending mental energy on it
- Find reliable protein sources near her neighborhood

### Frustrations
- "I don't have time to spend 90 minutes at the gym every day."
- "I know what I need to eat but the apps don't have Egyptian food — I have to estimate everything."
- "I need someone to tell me WHEN to eat and WHEN to train based on my schedule."
- "I hate apps that feel cheap and buggy — it makes me trust them less."

### Technology Behavior
- Primary device: iPhone 14 (iOS)
- Pays for Spotify, Netflix — has no problem paying for quality apps
- Values design and polish above everything
- Leaves App Store reviews (positive and negative)
- Early adopter; shares apps with colleagues

### What FitX Solves for Sara
- 20-min workout plans that actually fit her schedule
- Pro macro tracking with Egyptian food database
- AI form correction during home workouts
- Biological clock training: trains at optimal times for fat loss
- Smooth, premium UI that matches her iPhone quality expectations

### Quote
> "عايزة حاجة تاخد من دماغي قرار 'هتتمري امتى وتاكلي إيه' — أنا بس أنفذ."

## Persona 3: Coach Khaled — "المدرب" (The Trainer)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  👤 Khaled Ibrahim, 35 years old                                 │
│  Personal Trainer — World Gym, Maadi                             │
│  Trainer Account (Pro Extended)                                  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Background
Khaled has been a personal trainer for 9 years. He has 25 regular clients and manages their programs via WhatsApp groups, which is chaotic and unscalable. He's technically capable (uses Excel to track client progress) and is looking for a tool that helps him appear more professional and manage clients more efficiently.

### Goals
- Manage all client progress in one place
- Send personalized workout plans digitally
- Monitor client consistency (did they actually train?)
- Attract new clients by appearing more tech-forward

### Frustrations
- "I send the same workout plan to different clients on WhatsApp and I lose track of who's doing what."
- "Clients say they trained but I have no way to verify."
- "It's hard to adjust programs without seeing their logs."

### Technology Behavior
- Primary device: Samsung Galaxy S22
- Intermediate tech user
- Prefers simple dashboards over complex analytics
- Would recommend FitX to clients if it makes his job easier

### What FitX Solves for Khaled
- Assign workout plans to clients directly in-app (v2 feature)
- View client session logs and consistency reports
- Clients' progress visible in trainer dashboard
- Professional image boost: "My trainer uses FitX"

## Persona 4: Abu Hassan — "الشريك المحلي" (The Local Partner)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  👤 Hassan Mahmoud, 48 years old                                 │
│  Butcher Shop Owner — Shubra, Cairo                              │
│  Merchant Partner                                                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Background
Hassan has run his butcher shop for 20 years. He sells quality cuts but competition from supermarkets is increasing. His son suggested he try digital marketing but Facebook ads were expensive and brought random traffic. He wants customers who actually care about food quality and health.

### Goals
- Attract health-conscious, loyal customers
- Reduce slow-sales days with targeted offers
- Compete with supermarkets on quality, not just price

### Frustrations
- "Facebook ads cost me 500 EGP and I got 3 calls, none of them bought."
- "I don't know how to do digital marketing."
- "I want customers who care about clean meat, not just the cheapest."

### Technology Behavior
- Primary device: Samsung A32
- Basic smartphone user
- Son helps him with digital tools
- Will use FitX dashboard if it's simple; will abandon if complex

### What FitX Solves for Abu Hassan
- Highly targeted audience (fitness users who need protein)
- Simple offer creation (photo + discount % + duration)
- Commission-only model: zero risk, pay only when sales happen
- Rating system creates accountability (motivates quality)

## Persona 5: Mohammed — "المايسترو" (The Maestro / Admin)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  👤 Mohammed (Founder)                                           │
│  Founder & Tech Lead — FitX HQ                                   │
│  Admin Account (Full Access)                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Background
Mohammed is the founder and lead developer of FitX. He built the product from the ground up and knows every corner of the system. He needs an admin panel that gives him full control without requiring him to touch the database directly.

### Goals
- Monitor app health and growth metrics in real-time
- Maintain food and exercise database quality
- Manage merchant partner quality at scale
- Make data-driven decisions quickly

### Daily Admin Tasks
- Review new merchant partner applications
- Update weekly food prices
- Add new exercises with proper descriptions
- Monitor crash rate and API latency dashboards
- Review flagged content and user disputes

### Quote
> "من ورا الستار، بشوف كل حاجة وبحرك الكل."
